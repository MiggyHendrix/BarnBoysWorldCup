📋 HOW IT WORKS

The Buy-In

Each of the 24 players puts in $50, creating a $1,200 pot. The winner takes everything remaining after underdog bonuses are paid out.

The Draft

  • 48 teams split into two tiers: Top 24 (favorites) and Bottom 24 (underdogs) by FIFA power rankings.
  • Each player spins the wheel and gets one team from each tier — randomly paired.
  • No team is repeated. Every spin locks in a unique combo.

Winning the Pot

Your Top 24 team is your main horse. If they win the tournament, you take whatever remains in the pot after underdog bonuses are paid out.

🟢 Underdog Bonus

Every time your Bottom 24 team wins a knockout match (Round of 32 onward), you earn $25 from the pot. Group stage wins don't count. Bonuses are paid immediately.
